Sprint Planning Process

Sprint Planning Meeting
Agenda:
  1. Review previous sprint retrospective
  2. Present new features/user stories
  3. Break down tasks and estimate effort
  4. Assign tasks to team members
  5. Set sprint goals and success criteria
Duration: 2-4 hours
Frequency: Every 3 weeks
Task Estimation
Story Points:
  • 1 point: Simple tasks (Â½ day)
  • 2 points: Moderate tasks (1 day)
  • 3 points: Complex tasks (1-2 days)
  • 5 points: Epic tasks (2-3 days)
  • 8 points: Major features (3+ days)
Team Capacity:
20-30 points per sprint

Team Communication & Standups

Daily Standup
15-Minute Daily Check-in
  1. What did you do yesterday?
  2. What will you do today?
  3. Are there any blockers?
Format: In-person or video call
Time: Same time each day
Communication Tools
  • Slack/Discord: Daily communication
  • GitHub Issues: Task tracking
  • Video Calls: Sprint meetings
  • Documentation: Project wiki

Sprint Review & Retrospective

Sprint Review
Demo Completed Work
  • Demonstrate new features
  • Show working functionality
  • Present to stakeholders
  • Gather feedback and input
Duration: 30-60 minutes
Format: Live demo + Q&A
Sprint Retrospective
Process Improvement
  • What went well?
  • What could be improved?
  • What should we stop doing?
  • What should we start doing?
Duration: 45-60 minutes
Format: Open discussion
